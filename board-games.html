9 Board Games

9.1 Playing Online

9.1.1 RocketCrab

Open source platform (on GitHub!) to play mostly part games such as Avalon, Secret Hitler, A Fake Artist Goes to New York. Some of these games require additional tools, e.g. A Fake Artist Goes to New York only doles out roles and doesn’t handle the actual drawing. Bring your own pens!

9.1.2 BoardGameArena

A lightweight web app with a thriwing community. I am a reviewer on the site and the pipeline of new games is amazing.

9.1.3 Tabletopia

Tabletopia offers more newer games compared to BoardGameArena and their implementation is more of a 3D simulation of the game. It’s more resource intense too, but worth trying!
